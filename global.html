Founding Background

With the booming of new digital economy and the deepening of economic globalization,it has become a common understanding of the world's major countries to reshape the global industrial chain, supply chain and value chain with digital technology and to revitalize the global economy and promote new economic momentum. Integrating into the global supply chain competition system became the only way for global enterprises to strengthen global competitiveness.

As the pioneer in China, the town is well positioned. The town has "five themes" including global trading, presentation, distribution, innovation and service. We are committed to building a modern digital service industry with supply chain digitization technology, supply chain financial innovation and supply chain comprehensive services as the main industries. At the same time, according to the model of "guided by the government, driven by the market, led by well-known enterprises," we build a unique town of the digital economy in the modern service industry with new industries, new models and new business forms.

The global supply chain town is the offline global supply chain innovation and application base built by Zber and the regional government.

The History
of the Town

At present,
Zber serves as the first leader group.

October 2015

At the witness of Chinese premier Li Keqiang and German chancellor Angela Merkel, the SAP-Zber global supply chain trading cooperation base (Yangtze River delta) was officially settled in Nanjing, the capital of Jiangsu province.

January 2016

The global supply chain town, which is the SAP-Zber global supply chain trading cooperation base (Yangtze River delta), was officially launched.

June 2016

Zber global supply chain trading cooperation platform was officially operated.

October 7, 2017

The living hall of the global supply chain town was officially opened. This is the first new model of a town featured by the innovative application of digital supply chain in China.
