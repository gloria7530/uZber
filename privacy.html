Zber.com Privacy Statement


Welcome to visit and use Zber.com. The following is the Privacy Statement when you browse and use Zber.com. Once you have accessed and used Zber.com, it stands for your agreement for Zber.com to obtain, use and disclose your information in accordance with this Privacy Statement. The following may be subject to change due to the availability of more new services in the future without further notice. If you do not agree with the modified Privacy Statement, you may no longer access and use Zber.com. If you continue to access and use Zber.com, it is deemed that you have accepted the modified Privacy Statement. Therefore, it is recommended that you periodically review the Privacy Statement.

1. What user-related information will be collected by Zber.com?

User Information: Zber.com mainly collects the information the user has left on Zber.com, including but not limited to the company name, address, contact, telephone number, fax number, e-mail address and other company, product, and purchasing information submitted by the user. The information is collected for the purpose of executing Zber.com’s terms of service and providing users with the promised services so that potential partners or Zber.com’s staff can make timely contact with users, facilitate the conclusion of trade cooperation opportunities, and solve possible problems.

Hidden Data: In addition to the above user information, Zber.com also collects hidden information that can be referenced or used by Zber.com and its business partners. This type of information is mainly derived from “User access record”, including but not limited to “Cookies”, IP addresses, and so on. Zber.com collects this information in order to make Zber.com’s web-interface more user-friendly, and provide more targeted and effective services to users through comprehensive statistics and market analysis. It is also a necessary measure to prevent Zber.com from malicious attacks. To access Zber.com, your browser must be configured to accept cookies.

Other Information: In order to provide users with more comprehensive, professional and meticulous services, Zber.com will provide services for users through cooperation with third parties in certain fields. When the user chooses to apply a third-party’s service, the user may need to provide certain information for the third party, such as providing bank card information for an online payment institution.

2. How will Zber.com use your information?

The company name, address, contact, telephone number and other information released by the user on Zber.com for trade contact needs will be directly or indirectly displayed on Zber.com’s related web pages, and be open to all Zber.com users, so that potential partners can get in touch with users in a timely manner to facilitate the conclusion of trade cooperation. Therefore, Zber.com advises users to be cautious when deciding to release any information.

At the same time, Zber.com reserves the right to provide the partners with the result of comprehensive analysis of the information submitted by the user, but only provides the result of comprehensive analysis for Zber.com partners.

3. What are the responsibilities of users and Zber.com in protecting privacy?

Zber.com will do its utmost to ensure the stability and security of the information submitted by the users on Zber.com, but will not assume any risk resulting from the particularities of network transmission, nor will it make any kind of guarantee for it. Zber.com will disclose the user’s information in good faith under the following circumstances:

(1) The need to execute the Zber.com’s terms of services;

(2) In the case of protecting and safeguarding the legitimate rights and interests of Zber.com, other users of Zber.com and the general public;

(3) When Zber.com cooperates with a third party to provide better services for the user, but the third party will be immediately prohibited from accessing the relevant information after the cooperation comes to an end;

(4) The corresponding law or government requires Zber.com to provide relevant information in cooperation;

(5) Other circumstances where Zber.com needs to disclose user information.

At the same time, users should also be cautious with all the information submitted and released by you on Zber.com, especially personal information.

4. Disclaimer

Zber.com does not assume any responsibility for the privacy protection of other websites that are accessed through links contained in Zber.com.

Meanwhile, users should also keep their own usernames and passwords (if any) to log on the Zber.com website properly. Zber.com will not be responsible for the theft of usernames and passwords due to reasons not related to Zber.com.

Notes: Zber.com and Beijing Zber Technology Co., Ltd. reserve the right to interpret, modify and update this Privacy Statement. If you have any questions during the use of Zber.com, please consult the relevant service staff in time.
